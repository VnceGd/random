Manage Local Storage in Browser Settings

The games listed on the homepage use local storage to save data.
Blocking cookies disables this feature.

  • 'Snake' and 'Yahtzee' use your browser's local storage to save high scores.
  • 'Blackjack' stores a balance of (fake) money.

    Manage Local Storage using Inspector Tool

    A more direct method involves using the Inspector Tool and deleting items from local storage.

    Google Chrome:

    1. Right click on the web page and click 'Inspect', or press Ctrl + Shift + I.
    2. Click on the 'Application' tab on the panel that appears.
    3. Click on the 'Local Storage' dropdown (under 'Storage'), then click the url under it.
    4. Click on any item in the 'Key/Value' list and click the X or press Backspace/Delete to delete it.

    Mozilla Firefox:

    1. Right click on the web page and click 'Inspect Element', or press Ctrl + Shift + I.
    2. Click on the 'Storage' tab on the panel that appears.
    3. Click on the 'Local Storage' dropdown, then click the URL under it.
    4. Right click on any item in the 'Key/Value' list and click 'Delete [key]' or 'Delete All'.
